Mindwell Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including:

1.1. Personal Data

Personal data refers to any information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, and phone number.

1.2. Non-Personal Data

Non-personal data includes information that does not personally identify you, such as your browsing patterns or device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. To Provide and Improve Our Services

We use your information to provide and maintain our services and improve the functionality of our website.

2.2. To Communicate with You

We may contact you regarding updates, promotions, or inquiries you have submitted.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties, except as described in this policy.

4. Your Rights

4.1. Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to correct any inaccuracies.

4.2. Data Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal data under certain circumstances.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website.
